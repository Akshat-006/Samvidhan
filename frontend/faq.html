Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Parliament Question Management Portal

To submit a question, first login to your account. Then navigate to the dashboard and click on "Upload Question". Fill out the question form with your query, select the relevant department, and attach any supporting documents if needed. Once submitted, you can track the status of your question from the dashboard.

The response time varies depending on the complexity of the question and the department's current workload. Typically, you can expect an initial response within 5-7 working days. You can always check the status of your question in real-time through your dashboard.

Our system supports the following file formats for attachments:
  • PDF documents (.pdf)
  • Word documents (.doc, .docx)
  • Image files (.jpg, .png)
  • Text files (.txt)
The maximum file size allowed is 10MB per attachment.

Once a question is submitted, it cannot be modified directly. However, if you need to make changes to a pending question, please contact the admin through the support system. For questions that have already been processed or answered, you'll need to submit a new question with the updated information.
